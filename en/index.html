About Us · 01

A heritage kept
with care.

"The vine leaf is a delicate inheritance from nature. Bringing it to the table in its healthiest form is our responsibility."

— ESN Gıda · Production Philosophy

ESN Gıda & Konserve was founded in 2018 in Sivas, Turkey, specialising in the production of pickled vine leaves. Operating from our 3,600 m² enclosed production facility within a 10,000 m² area in the Sivas Ahmetturangazi Organised Industrial Zone, we are among Turkey's leading vine leaf producers with a daily capacity of 8,000 kg and a monthly capacity of 150,000 kg.

Vine leaves harvested from the fertile lands of the Tokat region are carefully washed, sorted, brined and packaged at our modern facility. Our production process is conducted under ISO 9001, ISO 22000, HACCP and BRC standards, with strict adherence to hygiene and traceability principles.

Through our three brands — ESN Gıda, Misbağ and Sarı Salkım — we serve major EU markets including Germany, Netherlands and Denmark, as well as the United States and Australia, while expanding into the Arabian Peninsula, Turkic Republics and Russia.

Production Philosophy · 03

From vineyard to table,
a meticulous chain.

Each vine leaf enters our traceability chain the moment it is harvested. Our production runs in four core stages, with every step tied to a quality control point and a hygiene discipline.

01
Harvest
Hand-picked from Tokat vineyards within season.
02
Sorting
Size and quality grading, twin-stage washing.
03
Brining
Natural brine for the leaf to mature.
04
Packaging
Controlled fill into PET, vacuum or glass.
