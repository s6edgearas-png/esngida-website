Cookie Policy.

Detailed information about the cookies used on esngida.com.tr, their purposes and how you can manage your preferences.

What are Cookies · 01

What is a cookie?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by the websites you visit, through your browser. They help the site function correctly, remember your preferences and measure site performance.

Cookie Types · 02

Which cookies we use.

Essential Cookies

Required for the basic functionality of the site (language selection, session management, form submission). Without these cookies the site cannot deliver its core services and they cannot be switched off.

Analytics Cookies

Used to understand how many people visit each page, how visitors arrive at our site and how it performs. Our site uses Netlify Analytics, which runs on the server side and therefore does not place cookies in your browser. If we add a third-party analytics tool in the future (such as Google Analytics) we will request your consent separately.

Marketing Cookies

Used for advertising and remarketing. We do not currently use any marketing cookies on our site; if any are added in the future we will obtain your explicit consent.

Manage Preferences · 03

How to manage cookies.

You can change your cookie preferences through your browser settings, delete existing cookies and block new ones. Common browser instructions:

  • Google Chrome: Settings > Privacy and security > Cookies
  • Mozilla Firefox: Settings > Privacy & Security > Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences > Privacy > Cookies and website data
  • Microsoft Edge: Settings > Cookies and site permissions

Please note that disabling essential cookies may prevent some parts of the site from working correctly.
