Hygiene & Quality · 03

Every batch controlled,
every batch tested.

Our line operates with 12 critical control points defined under HACCP. Every production batch is subjected to physicochemical and microbiological testing; results are archived alongside the traceability log of the product. Raw material intake, brine pH, filling temperature and final cap pressure are the core checkpoints in this chain.
